Column Name,Description
country,Name of the country
child_mort,Death of children under 5 years of age per 1000 live births
exports,Exports of goods and services. Given as %age of the Total GDP
health,Total health spending as %age of Total GDP
imports,Import of goods and services. Given as %age of the Total GDP
Income,Net income per person
Inflation,The measurement of the annual growth rate of the Total GDP
life_expec,The average number of years a new born child would live if the current mortality patterns are to remain the same
total_fel,The number of children that would be born to each woman if the current age-fertility rates remain the sae.
gdpp,The GDP percapita. Calculated as the Total GDP divided by the total population.
